# Kit: Brand Expression
**Domain:** brand-expression
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Status:** draft

## Requirements

### R-001: Opinionated product personality
UI must communicate a clear product point of view rather than a neutral utility aesthetic. Tone, naming, and interface copy must make product feel authored and intentional.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- AC-1: Primary UI surfaces use a consistent voice in labels, empty states, helper text, and status messages.
- AC-2: Interface avoids placeholder or generic enterprise wording on primary screens.
- AC-3: At least 1 persistent brand or product-signature element appears in normal app use without obstructing workflow.

### R-002: Terminal ritual moments
Product must include memorable terminal-native moments that create identity without slowing expert use. These moments must reinforce craft, confidence, and differentiation.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- AC-1: Startup, screen transitions, or key state changes include at least 1 recognizable product-signature interaction or presentation pattern.
- AC-2: Signature moments complete quickly enough that they do not block user input during normal startup or navigation.
- AC-3: Signature moments are consistent with terminal constraints and remain legible in text-only environments.

### R-003: Functional polish over novelty
Brand expression must support usability first. Identity moments must not reduce clarity, obscure state, or compete with active work.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- AC-1: Brand elements never hide active task state, input affordances, or error information.
- AC-2: Decorative treatments can be visually distinguished from actionable controls.
- AC-3: Removing brand-only decoration would not change workflow logic, available actions, or required user decisions.

## Out of Scope
- Company-wide rebrand outside terminal app
- Mascots, illustrations, or non-terminal media systems
- Audio, animation-heavy, or splash-screen-first experiences
